Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026.
Beta version: this is a working draft of the privacy policy. The final version will be confirmed by a legal advisor before public launch.

Who we are

Querencia is an app for emotional support among women. The data controller is Split Solutions d.o.o. (Croatia). For any privacy questions, write to dev@split-solutions.com.

What data we collect

Data you enter yourself: email and password (for sign-in), your name, optionally a city and a few words about yourself, your interests, and the content you post — text, voice recordings, messages, reactions and moods. From your phone settings we read the language and country (e.g. "HR"), without GPS and without your location. Technically we collect a notification token, the basic data needed for the app to work, and anonymous technical error logs.

Content personalization

We tailor the content in the app (posts and suggested women) to you: based on the interests you chose, the circles you take part in, your posts, notes and reactions, and who you interact with in the app. This helps us gauge what feels close to you and show it first. The content of your private messages is NOT used for this. Personalization has no legal or similarly significant effects — only the order in which things are shown — and is not used for advertising.

Why we use them

To let you sign in, connect you with women and topics, show your feed and conversations, send the notifications you allowed, and keep the community safe. We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell it.

Where they are stored

Data is stored with our infrastructure provider (Supabase) on servers within the EU (Frankfurt, Germany). Access is limited to authorized persons on a need-to-know basis.

Our service providers (data processors)

To run the app we use trusted service providers that process data on our instructions and under a data processing agreement: Supabase (database and storage, servers in the EU — Frankfurt), Expo/EAS (delivery of notifications and app updates; USA, under the EU Standard Contractual Clauses) and Google Firebase (technical delivery of notifications to Android devices). None of them use your data for their own purposes.

Who we share them with

Your profile and posts are visible to other members of the community, in line with the app's settings. We do not share personal data with third parties other than the service providers necessary to run the app, or when required by law.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct and delete your data, and to object. You can permanently delete your account and all data yourself in the app: Profile → Delete account. For other rights, contact dev@split-solutions.com.

How long we keep them

We keep your data while you have an account. Deleting your account permanently deletes your data (posts, messages, voice recordings, gestures and profile).

Age

Querencia is intended for adult women (18+). It is not for children.
